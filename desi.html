Venue


The meeting will be conducted at the Faculté de Droit et de Science Politique of Aix-Marseille Université. The campus is located at 110-114 La Canebière (Google Maps)

The rooms we will be using are:
  • Emerigon for plenary (3rd floor)
  • Jourdan
  • Boulouis
  • Poinso-Chapuis


ECS Accommodation


The hotel booked for the Early Career Scientist who have obtained the DESI ECS grant is the IBIS Budget Hotel , located at: 46 rue Sainte, 13001, Marseille, France (Google Maps)

Conference dinner


The conference dinner will be held at the UNM Restaurant, located at: 34 Bd Charles Livon, 13007, Marseille (Google Maps)
